curries

Indian flatbread known for its smokey taste and soft, chewy texture is called tabooni roti. This unleavened bread is made by baking dough in a tandoor, a cylindrical clay oven. The high cooking temperature produces an appealingly soft inside and a slightly burnt outside. Its versatility makes it the ideal side dish for a broad range of curries, kebabs, and sauces because of its simplicity. With each delicious mouthful, tandoori roti, with its alluring scent and adaptable texture, continues to be a cherished mainstay of Indian cooking.
